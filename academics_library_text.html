The Albion College Library provides access to more than 400,000 books and other non-print items, over 19,600 online and print journal subscriptions, and full-text articles from more than 50,000 journals through the library's many databases. OneSearch, the library's discovery service, can be used to locate information on any topic from all our collections with a single search.

The Stockwell Memorial Library building houses the Wendell Will Faculty Room, the new Cutler Commons, and Read Between the Grinds café.
